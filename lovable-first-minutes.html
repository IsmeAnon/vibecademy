🧭 Your First 5 Minutes with Vibe Coding

🧸 Buddy says: “Follow these pictures. In 5 minutes, you'll have built something real. No code. No fear. Just your idea.”

1. Go to Lovable

Click this link: lovable.dev (opens in a new tab).

📸 This is the Lovable homepage. Click the link, then look for the “Get started” button.

2. Sign up for free

You’ll need a free account to save your work. Sign up with Google, GitHub, or email – it takes 30 seconds.

3. Type your idea

After signing in, you’ll see a big text box. Type something simple, like:

“A welcome page for my family”

📸 Your idea, ready to build.

4. Click “Build”

Press the green Build button. The AI will create your page in a few seconds.

📸 Click the green button.

5. See your creation

Your page will appear on the right. You can share it, change it, or keep building.

📸 Your first vibe‑coded project! (This example is a dashboard – yours might be a welcome page.)

6. Share your creation

Click the Share button (usually top right). Copy the link and send it to family or friends.

🌟 Real example: Here’s what Anon built with this guide: Family Welcome Page. You can make yours too!

🎉 That’s it! You’ve just built something with AI. Now explore, make changes, and share with your loved ones. You’re a creator.
